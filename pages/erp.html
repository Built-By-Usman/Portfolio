Silent Salah

Stay focused in Salah – we silence your phone at the right time.

Key Features:

  • 🕌 Auto-Silent Mode: Mutes phone at each prayer time
  • 🔔 Auto-Restore Sound: Unmutes phone after prayer ends
  • 📅 Accurate Timings: Based on your location and daily updates
  • 📱 Background Service: Works even when app is closed
  • ⚙️ Simple Setup: One-time permission, no manual control needed

Tech Stack:

Kotlin, Foreground Services, Animations and Alarm Manager
